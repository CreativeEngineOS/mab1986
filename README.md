# MAB 1986 Baseball Season Manager

## The Concept

*It is 1986. The regular season is coming to an end, but a heavy rainy day has brought the real games to a halt.* 

*Rain Delay is called, the only way to keep the baseball spirit alive is to boot up the vintage console and relive the highlights of the season.*

## What is MAB 1986 Baseball Season Manager?

Welcome to **MAB 1986 Baseball Season Manager** — a game that perfectly encapsulates that nostalgic 8-bit retro vibe while serving as a dynamic dashboard for simulating and managing the rest of your season. It is time to time-travel through your team's historical games, manually play out upcoming post-season matchups, track advanced metrics, and secure the championship.

## Features

- **Retro Aesthetic**: 8-bit visual cues reminiscent of '80s computing, including dynamic CRT scanlines and classic typography.
- **Time Travel**: Traverse historical matchups to see how records, stats, and Elo power ratings shifted over the course of the season.
- **Authentic Match Simulator**: Score tickers simulate innings with random intervals, and unplayed matches sit on the timeline ready for you to input final scores and attach unique broadcast notes (e.g., "Grand slam by David!").
- **Double-Elimination Post-Season**: After completing the 14-game regular season, advance to the 19-game Double Elimination playoff bracket. The game automatically calculates seeds, and dynamically updates upcoming matchups based on your inputted final scores!
- **Dynamic Dashboard**: Deep analytical stats including Cumulative Run Differential, Runs Scored vs Runs Allowed, and Pythagorean True Win %.
- **Gamepad Support**: Kick back with a controller—just like the old days. Use your gamepad joystick/D-pad to select teams, 'A' to play/save, 'L2/R2' to navigate the timeline, and 'L1/R1' to toggle charts. Press the 'Triangle' (Top Face Button) to seamlessly toggle focus back and forth between teams in a matchup card without losing your chronological place!
