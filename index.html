How to Play & Features

Time Travel: Click any game node on the bottom timeline to travel back in time. Standings, Elo records, narratives, and charts instantly adapt to show the season state at that exact moment.

Play/Post-Season: Unplayed games are dashed. Select them to enter manual scores and play out the rest of the season.

Broadcast Notes: When entering manual scores, add custom game notes (e.g., "Grand slam by David!"). These notes are injected into the post-game narrative headlines.

Season Notebook: Access the "Season Notebook" from the main menu to view all your custom broadcast notes in a chronological feed.

Dashboard Tabs: Click the tabs above the chart to switch visualizations: Game Diff (game margin), Cum Diff (cumulative run margin), PF vs PA (runs scored vs runs allowed), and Elo Trend (Elo strength over time).

Educational Tooltips: Hover over key statistics (like Elo power score or Trend) to see tooltips explaining how they are calculated.

Gamepad Controls: Play with any connected controller! Press A to play/save, Left/Right to cycle teams, L2/R2 to travel the timeline, L1/R1 to change chart tabs, and Square for Standings.
